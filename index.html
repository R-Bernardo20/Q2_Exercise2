Science Topics

Momentum

a measurement of mass in motion: how much mass is in how much motion. It is usually given the symbol P

Impulse

a term that quantifies the overall effect of a force acting over time. It is conventionally given the symbol J and expressed in Newton-seconds.

Valence Electrons

Outermost shell in the electron cloud (has the highest energy level)

Number of valence electrons is based on the group number
